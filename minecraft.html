CRAFTING

Weapons and Mining essentials

Item Materials Use
Sword A stick, and 2 of a material(stone, wood, diamond, iron, gold) Good for killing mobs.
Shovel 2 sticks, and a material Good for digging dirt
Pickaxe 2 sticks, and 3 of a material Good for mining stone and stuff
Axe 2 sticks, and 3 of a material Good for cutting wood
Hoe 2 sticks, and 2 of a material Good for getting ground ready for crops
Shears 2 Iron Good for collecting leaves and wool

Armor

Item Materials("Material" is iron, gold, leather, or diamond) Use
Helmet 5 of a material Provides protection from mob damage. Also you look cool
Chestplate 8 of a material Provides protection from mob damage. Also you look cool
Leggings 7 of a material Provides protection from mob damage. Also you look cool
Boots 4 of a material Provides protection from mob damage. Also you look cool

Other crafts

Item Materials Use
Bed 3 wool of any type, 3 wood planks Sets a respawn point, and you can sleep throught the night
Crafting Table 4 wood planks So you can craft a lot more things
Furnace 8 stone or cobblestone Cook things, make charcoal, smelt
Chest 8 wood planks Store things
Wood planks 1 wood makes 4 planks For A LOT of things
Sticks 2 wood makes 4 sticks For ladders, tools, and others
Ladder 7 sticks makes 3 ladders So you can climb up vertical holes
Door 6 wood makes 3 doors To protect your house, and to have a door
Trapdoor 6 wood makes 3 trapdoors Basically flat doors, can go on the roof of your house, or entrance to mines
Torch 1 stick and 1 coal or charcoal makes 4 torches To light up the place
Pressure plate 2 of a material When you step on it, something chosen activates
Button 1 of a material Mobs can't spawn on buttons.
Ingots 1 of a material(smelt it). It has to be iron or gold or something To make armor and tools
