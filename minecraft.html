CRAFTING

Weapons and Mining essentials

Item Materials Instructions Use
Sword A stick, and 2 of a material(stone, wood, diamond, iron, gold) Place the stick in the bottom center slot, and the two materials on top of it, stacked not in the same slot, but on top of eachother Good for killing mobs.
Shovel 2 sticks, and a material Place the two sticks on top of eachoter, the bottom one in the bottom center slot, and the material on top of them. Good for digging dirt
Pickaxe 2 sticks, and 3 of a material First start with the same placing as the shovel, then add two materials: one in the top right corner, one in the top left. Good for mining stone and stuff
Axe 2 sticks, and 3 of a material First start with the same placing as the shovel, then add two materials, one in the top left corner, and the other under that one. Good for cutting wood
Hoe 2 sticks, and 2 of a material First start with the same start as the shovel, then add one more material, in the top left corner. Good for getting ground ready for crops
Shears 2 Iron ingots Place on iron ingot in the bottom left corner, and the other in the center Good for collecting leaves and wool

Armor

Item Materials("Material" is iron, gold, leather, or diamond) Instructions Use
Helmet 5 of a material Completely cover the top row with the material, then place one material in the center left, and one in the center right Provides protection from mob damage. Also you look cool
Chestplate 8 of a material Completely cover the bottom and the center row with the material, then place one material in the top left, and one in the top right Provides protection from mob damage. Also you look cool
Leggings 7 of a material Cover the top row with the material, then the left and right columns with that same material Provides protection from mob damage. Also you look cool
Boots 4 of a material Place the material in the top left and top right corners, then the center left and center right slots. Provides protection from mob damage. Also you look cool

Other crafts

Item Materials Instructions Use
Bed 3 wool of any type, 3 wood planks Cover the top row with wool, then the center row with wood planks. Sets a respawn point, and you can sleep throught the night
Crafting Table 4 wood planks Make a 2 by 2 rectangle with the wood planks So you can craft a lot more things
Furnace 8 stone or cobblestone Cover the borders with cobblestone Cook things, make charcoal, smelt
Chest 8 wood planks Cover the borders with wood planks Store things
Wood planks 1 wood makes 4 planks Place a wood log in the center For A LOT of things
Sticks 2 wood planks make 4 sticks Place one wood plank in the center, and one in the bottom center For ladders, tools, and others
Ladder 7 sticks makes 3 ladders Make an H shape with the sticks(cover left and right columns with sticks, then one stick in the center) So you can climb up vertical holes
Door 6 wood makes 3 doors Cover the 1st and 2nd columns with wood planks To protect your house, and to have a door
Trapdoor 6 wood makes 3 trapdoors Cover the 1st and 2nd rows with wood planks Basically flat doors, can go on the roof of your house, or entrance to mines
Torch 1 stick and 1 coal or charcoal makes 4 torches Place the stick in the bottom center slot, and the charcoal or coal in the center slot. To light up the place
Charcoal 1 wooden log, some feul, and a furnace. BURN THE WOODEN LOG!!! Used to make torches
Pressure plate 2 of a material Place the 2 materials next to eachother, horizantally. When you step on it, something chosen activates
Button 1 of a material Literally just place the material somewhere. Mobs can't spawn on buttons.
Ingots 1 of a material and a furnace. It has to be raw iron or raw gold or something BURN THE MATERIAL!!! To make armor and tools
