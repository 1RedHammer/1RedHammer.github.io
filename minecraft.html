The Basics

Minecraft is a fun game where you mine, craft, build, slaughter, and use your brain. It's the most played video game, ever. Buy it on amazon or something, and play! To move, use WASD. To mine, hold left-click. To place a block, select it using keys 1-9, and right-click to place it. To open doors, trapdoors, fill a bucket, untie a leash, tie a leash, and others, right-click. In fact, right-click is for most actions. To craft, open a chest, and even cook stuff in your furnace. Anyway, e to open your inventory, click, and click again to move things, and use ctrl+q to drop things. T to open chat, where you chat with everybody else in your server. The goal of the game (in survival mode) is to defeat the Ender Dragon, who's probably the worst boss ever (it has no phases), but is still a real challenge. You have to travel to the Nether(make a portal to the nether by making a rectangular shape with obsidian, leaving the inside blank. The frame can be anywhere from 4x5 to 23x23 blocks in size. Then, summon lightning inside of it using flint and steel, and boom. There's a portal.) , and find it. Yeah. Mine, craft, build, and fight your way to victory!!!!!!!!!

MINING TECHNIQUES

When mining, it's always good to have a mine. To make one, dig down, but not straight down, to about y position 15 (You can use "show coordinates" in settings). That's where diamond starts to generate. Start mining. Later on, when you have a lot of stone, go back up, and make like WAY TOO MANY torches, and a lot of stone pickaxes too. Then, just go back down to your mine, and start mining like crazy. When all your pickaxes break, You should maybe have some iron, so then, go back up and make an iron pickaxe. With that, you can start mining gold, diamond, and redstone. With those, you can make more tools(but not redstone). Later, you can dig all the way down to bedrock, which is the unbreakable material at the bottom of minecraft. It's usually at about y position -59. Start making long tunnels about 3 blocks above it. Just make straight tunnels, 2 blocks high, 1 block wide. If you want, you can also make some passages, branching off the main passage. Make those smaller tunnels about 2 blocks apart.

When you have very few iron or diamond tools, make sure you mine stone with stone pickaxes. Otherwise, your iron and diamond could break, and then you wouldn't be able to mine redstone, gold, and diamond. Diamond looks like stone with blue rectangles in it. Iron looks like stone with grey rectangles in it. Gold looks like stonee with gold rectangles in it. You get the idea. When you mine a lot, you get a LOT of stone. Every time you go back up to your house, you should store most of that stone in chests, otherwise stone could completely cover your entire inventory, and you wouldn't have enough room for stone pickaxes. There's a max of 64 stone in one slot, so if you mine, like, WAY too much, you could end up with thousands of stone. That would be good for making cool things(see Building Ideas)

BUILDING IDEAS

There are some very cool things and very essential things that you can build in minecraft-learn some here!

  • A house. Houses are probably the most useful things in minecraft. You could also build a bunker, but that would be harder to spot. Build a house by making a square with any material (I recommend stone or wood planks), and then make walls. Make walls by stacking more materials on top of the square. Make the height three, then fill in the top. When making the roof, always leave a hole, for a trapdoor. Then put ladders on the walls so you can climb up to the hold and go onto the roof. Later, you can add 2nd and 3rd floors, and make your house really cool, but first start with a basic house.
  • A bunker. Bunkers are also really useful, because you usually put beds in them, and they're underground. If you make a house, you should still add a bunker, because if your house gets taken over by mobs, you can run to your bunker. Make a bunker by digging a giant hole. Yeah.
  • An infinite cobblestone generator. These are REALLY cool. You can mine cobblestone without even going below the surface! All you have to do is make a bucket, and have a water source, and a lava source. First, dig a 1 block deep hole, and fill it with water. Then, dig a two block deep hole exactly right next to it, and that will fill with water too. Then, continuing in the same direction, skip one block and dig a one block deep hole after that. Fill that single hole with lava. Then, dig up the block that seperates the water and the lava, and cobblestone will start to generate. Cover the lava with cobblestone, otherwise you could fall in and die. If the lava turns into obsidian, then try again.
  • A trench. These are useful, but not so cool. Anyway, since mobs just fall into these holes, your house can become mostly mob-proof. All you have to do is dig a 2 block deep, 2 block wide hole around your house. Then you can put trapdoors over some parts, to walk across. Just remember to open those trapdoors when you aren't walking across, because otherwise, the mobs could also just walk across.
